## What Is Quantum Garden?

Quantum Garden is a reality-bending text-based game where players tend a garden that exists in multiple quantum states simultaneously. It combines quantum mechanics concepts with gardening simulation to create a meditative, philosophical gaming experience.

## Why Is It Unique?

### Never-Before-Seen Game Mechanics

1. **Real Quantum Superposition Gameplay**
   - Your garden literally exists in multiple realities at once
   - Not just a theme, but the actual core game mechanic
   - Each quantum state evolves independently

2. **Observer Effect Mechanics**
   - When you observe/collapse a state, all other realities cease to exist
   - Permanent, irreversible choices that mirror real quantum physics
   - Forces meaningful decision-making

3. **Reality Splitting**
   - Create new quantum superpositions from collapsed states
   - Branch timelines and explore parallel universes
   - Infinite replayability through quantum randomness

4. **Real-Time Persistent Evolution**
   - Garden tracks actual real-world time
   - Returns after hours/days to find evolved garden
   - Grows even when you're not playing

5. **Procedural Quantum ASCII Art**
   - Each quantum state generates unique visual patterns
   - Plant mutations create emergent designs
   - No two gardens are ever the same

6. **Philosophy-Driven Gameplay**
   - Meditation on observation, choice, and reality
   - No combat, no scoring, no traditional "winning"
   - Pure creative and contemplative experience
